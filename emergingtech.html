CONVERSATIONAL AI

Voice experiences are appearing everywhere. Smart speakers in the home, in your car, on your phone or at a kiosk in the store gives consumers the opportunity to interact in a personalized way with brands and products.

In this PDF we will address:

Virtual Assistants

Voice devices in the home account for the majority of experiences ranging from Smart Home to Kitchen Assistance.

ZERO UI

Assistants are built on speech recognition, so instead of swiping and typing, you can stay heads-up and hands-free while you multitask around the house.

BRAND VOICE

Using various tips and tricks brands can overcome the robotic feeling of a voice assistant and discover their own voice.

DEVELOPMENT

With a variety of native and platform solutions for voice and chat, VMLY&R can accommodate many sets of requirements and cross channel asks.
